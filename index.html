Welcome to the UTPrimers team.

UTPrimers is a non-profit extension project, linked to the Federal Technological University of Paraná - Campus Ponta Grossa and formed by 20 undergraduate students from the Bioprocess Engineering and Biotechnology, Biological Sciences, Chemical Engineering and Biotechnology courses.

Selective Process

We will open the selection process for new members at the beginning of March, the selection pprocess will be open for different iniversities

Competitions

We are a competition team in syntheic biology, some of the main competitions we participae in were: Igem Desingn League, Biodesign League

Researchs

As a team we developed some research in the area of biology for competitions or for internal development

Support us

We always need monney to pay for competition registrations, which are not cheap, and to buy material for the team to work
